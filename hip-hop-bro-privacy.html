Privacy Policy for Hip-Hop-Bro

Last Updated: 11-28-25

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **Hip-Hop-Bro** ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you use our mobile game.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do **NOT** collect any personal identification information such as your name, email address, or contact details.

Non-Personal Information

Through Google AdMob advertising services, the following non-personal information may be collected:

  • Advertising identifiers (Google Advertising ID)
  • Device type and operating system
  • General location data (country-level, not precise)
  • Advertisement interaction data

No Other Data Collection

We do not collect:

  • Game progress data
  • User profiles
  • In-app purchase information
  • Any other personal or sensitive data

How We Use Information

The non-personal information collected through AdMob is used solely for:

  • Displaying banner advertisements
  • Showing interstitial ads at natural break points
  • Providing reward videos for optional bonuses
  • Personalizing ad content (handled by AdMob)

Third-Party Services

Our game uses **Google AdMob** for:

  • Banner advertisements
  • Interstitial advertisements
  • Reward video advertisements

AdMob may collect and process information according to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do **NOT** sell, trade, or rent any user information. Non-personal data may be shared with:

  • Google AdMob and its advertising partners
  • As required by law or legal process

Your Rights and Choices

You can:

  • Reset your advertising identifier through device settings
  • Opt-out of personalized ads in device settings
  • Disable ad tracking in your device preferences

Children's Privacy

**Hip-Hop-Bro** does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our ads comply with Google's Families Policy requirements.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of **Hip-Hop-Bro** after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: media@fabstudiogames.com
