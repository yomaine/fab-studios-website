Privacy Policy Center

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fab Studios. This Privacy Policy governs our practices concerning data protection for our mobile games and website. Our mission is to craft fun and engaging experiences while respecting your privacy. This page serves as the main hub for all policies, including data handling, advertising, and terms of service.

2. Data We Collect

We primarily use anonymous data to improve game performance. We do not collect or store personal identifying information (PII) such as names, addresses, or emails from children. The data collected is limited to:

  • Non-personalized advertising identifiers (subject to platform controls).
  • Game progress and device language settings.
  • Crash reports and anonymous usage statistics.

3. Advertising and Monetization

Our games may contain contextual, non-personalized advertising served by Google AdMob, or other verified ad providers. We implement measures to comply with platform regulations, including COPPA and GDPR, by ensuring no personalized ads are served to users identified as children.

4. Contact Information

For any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please contact us at: media@fabstudiogames.com.
